Veteran are usually people who have served to any particular field for a very long time and have taken retirement currently are generally called has veterans. There are various types of veterans for example: Military Veteran and War Veteran are the most known veteran community in the public.

IDVA - Illinois Department of Veterans Affairs is the department that administrates the the veterans living in Illinois States. The dataset shown above gives us a detailed statistics of veterans in Illinois. Now lets us analyze the veterans per county by first 5 countys from the dataset. To analyze let use the chart built-in from idyll:

Interact with Data - Central Visualization

The dataset provides us various information about the veterans. The dataset has 6 different columns. They are as follows: county, females, males, veteranspercounty, withdependents, withoutdependents. But however in our article we will analyzing data of county, females, males and with veterans per county.

Let us analyze the data of county with veterans per county. But however we are also eager to know more about the statistics of number of females and number of males within the dependents by county list. Therefore we have implemented an interactive feature to the reader where he gets a chance to read the data by number of females or number of males or none. The data displaying the number of females or males will be shown with color map that distingues by strength. The reader gets to interact with the plot by clicking on the button.
